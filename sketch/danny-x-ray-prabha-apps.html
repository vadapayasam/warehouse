4. What Prabha Needs to Fix

A. The Revenue Gap

Two of your apps (Time Left, WhatPay) are completely free with no path to revenue. Danny's #1 lesson: charge more, remove free trials. You have built two apps in PROVEN revenue categories (subscription tracking, countdown widgets) and given them away.

B. The Distribution Gap

Danny spent 80%% of his energy on content, not code. He posted 1-2 faceless videos/day across 4 platforms. You have zero social presence. Your apps are invisible. The App Store does not market them for you.

C. The WhatPay Irony

Your app teaches people to track what they pay for subscriptions. Your app itself has no subscription price. This is not a bug. It is a philosophical failure. If you cannot charge for a subscription tracker, why would anyone trust your app to track theirs?

D. The Version Confusion

WhatPay v1.0 listed "Export all your data as CSV" as a feature. v1.1 removed that claim but still mentions iCloud sync. If the feature was removed, the App Store description is now misleading. Fix it.

Reality check: Danny's app made $130K because he charged for it from day one, marketed it aggressively, and iterated on what worked. Your apps might be better designed, but they are invisible and free. Design talent does not pay rent. Distribution and monetization do.

5. The Fix List

  1. Time Left: Add Pro tier ($2.99 one-time or $0.99/month). Premium widgets, unlimited events, custom themes.
  2. WhatPay: Add Pro tier ($1.99/week or $4.99/month). Multi-currency, bank sync, spending insights. Fix CSV claim in description.
  3. Leave Hack: Audit existing IAPs. If none exist, add Pro tier for advanced planning ($2.99).
  4. ThatsMyFont: Add Pro upgrade ($4.99 additional) for advanced typography features. Start making faceless demo videos.
  5. All apps: Start a TikTok/Reels account. Post 1 video/day showing the app's "magic moment."
  6. Content format: Screen recording + text overlay. No face needed. 15 seconds max.
Stop building features. Start charging money and making videos.
