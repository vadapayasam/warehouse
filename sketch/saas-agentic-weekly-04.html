Strategic Implications for SuperOps

🎯 Takeaways

What This Means

  • Agentic AI is the new baseline. Copilots and assistants are table stakes — autonomy is the differentiator.
  • Memory matters. Systems that learn from past incidents will outperform rules-based automation.
  • Multi-agent > single agent. Specialized agents working together beats one general-purpose chatbot.
  • Pricing pressure. PagerDuty's consumption model + seat pricing means SuperOps can compete on value, not just cost.
  • Integration gap exists. No PSA/billing native to PagerDuty — MSPs still need separate tools.

Opportunity: PagerDuty doesn't natively serve MSPs. The agentic capabilities + PSA integration gap = room for SuperOps AI Service Desk.
