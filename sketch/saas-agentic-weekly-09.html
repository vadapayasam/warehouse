Known Gaps

Enterprise DNA, MSP blocker

Ivanti Neurons is enterprise ITSM with AI agents, not an MSP platform. The gaps are structural, not fixable with a config change.

No PSA Integration

No native ConnectWise, Autotask, or Kaseya PSA connectors. Limited utility for MSPs running mixed stacks. The only ITSM bridge is to ServiceNow (a competitor). [7]

No Multi-Tenancy

Each client needs a separate instance. No MSP dashboard to view all clients. No cross-client reporting. This is a deal-breaker for any MSP with more than 5 clients. [7]

Quote-Based Pricing

No self-serve pricing, no per-client billing model. Implementation is a separate cost. AI add-on costs unknown (not yet disclosed). Cannot factor into MSP service pricing. [7]

Closed AI Ecosystem

AI agents only work within Ivanti Neurons. No MCP protocol support (unlike PagerDuty Advance). No cross-platform agentic AI. Can't participate in multi-vendor agent workflows. [2]

User complaints (third-party aggregators): Steep learning curve for configuration. Reporting capabilities less flexible than competitors. Customization requires Ivanti-specific scripting knowledge. Support quality varies by region. These are consistent with an enterprise product that optimizes for depth, not ease of use. [7]
