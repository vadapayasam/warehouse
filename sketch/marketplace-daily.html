Research Date: May 17, 2026 · Source: r/msp (183k members)

Key Insight from Reddit Analysis

  • Vendor Relationship Tools: MSPs struggle with vendor responsiveness & trade references—opportunity for vendor management/response automation
  • M365 Backup Alternatives: Strong demand for Backupify alternatives (Kaseya acquisition causing exodus)
  • HIPAA Compliance Automation: Medical practices ignore compliance—tools to enforce/audit would have buyers
  • Client Quality Scoring: MSPs need data on which industries/clients are "nightmare" vs profitable—currently tribal knowledge

Top Pain Points from r/msp

Client Fire Notifications Need tools to professionally exit bad clients with legal protection
Vendor Response Automation Trade references, support requests—vendors ghosting MSPs
Compliance Enforcement Medical/dental clients ignoring HIPAA; MSPs liable
Backup Tool Migration Leaving Backupify/Datto—need 9-year-old email recovery
