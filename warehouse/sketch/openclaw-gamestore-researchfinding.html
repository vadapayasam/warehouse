GameDev Market BANS ALL AI Assets

GameDev Market has implemented a blanket, platform-wide ban on generative AI assets. All sellers must certify 100% human creation with zero AI use. This eliminates one major marketplace for LLM-generated content. Focus on Unity Asset Store and itch.io instead.

✅

Unity Asset Store Allows AI — With Disclosure

Unity officially permits AI-generated content on the Asset Store, provided it doesn't resemble copyrighted work and is properly disclosed. This is the primary target marketplace for AI-generated UI packs.

📈

Glassmorphism/Liquid Glass UI is HOT Right Now

Apple's iOS 26 introduced Liquid Glass design language, creating massive demand for glassmorphism UI kits. Flutter packages like liquid_glass_widgets launched just 36 hours ago. Unity Asset Store has limited glassmorphism packs — this is a timing play while the trend is hot.

💰

Social Reward Effects Pack is a MASSIVE Gap

Zero dedicated "social effects" packs exist on Unity Asset Store. Individual heart animations sell for $5-$15, but there's no comprehensive pack for hearts/likes floating, notification pops, achievement unlocks, streak fire, leaderboard reveals. Casual/mobile games desperately need this.

📱

Notification/Toast UI Animation is Completely Missing

Searched Unity, GameDev Market, itch.io — zero dedicated notification toast animation packs. Found one Unity tool ($39) and one itch.io pack ($9.99), but both are basic. CSS/Lottie/SVG toast animations are natively LLM-generatable and high-demand for mobile games.

    Build Notification Toast Animation Pack

    Zero competition for dedicated toast/notification animation packs. CSS/Lottie/SVG toasts are natively LLM-generatable. Include success, error, warning, info states with slide-in, fade, bounce animations. Price: $8-$12.

  • 4
    Avoid GameDev Market — Focus on Unity + itch.io

    GameDev Market has banned ALL AI assets. Unity Asset Store allows AI with disclosure (70% revenue share). itch.io has no AI restrictions and offers 90%+ revenue share. Prioritize these two marketplaces.

  • 5
    Use URP-First Approach for Unity Packs

    Unity's Universal Render Pipeline (URP) is the modern standard for mobile/casual games. Marketing your pack as "URP-ready" or "URP-first" is an immediate differentiator. Most legacy packs are Built-in Render Pipeline only.

  • 6
    Build Portfolio of 10+ Packs Over 12-18 Months

    Don't expect one pack to generate meaningful income. Plan to release 10-15 packs across different categories (UI kits, VFX, icons, animations) over 12-18 months. Portfolio diversity = steady passive income.

  • 7
    Always Disclose AI Generation on Unity Store

    Unity requires disclosure for AI-generated content. Be transparent in your asset description: "Created with AI tools (Midjourney, ComfyUI) and refined in Photoshop." This builds trust and avoids potential takedowns.
